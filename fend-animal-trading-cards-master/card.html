Dolphin

The dolphin is a great swimmer and can leap up to 30ft in the air.

  • Habitat: Saltwater, Freshwater
  • Food: Fish, Octopus, Squid, Shrimp
  • Social Life: Hunting, Caring for young
  • Predators: Killer Whales, Sharks, Humans

The dolphin is a fun, well-liked, and joyful animal often featured in stories and movies. There are over 30 different species of dolphins, with the bottlenose dolphin being the most common. Dolphins use echolocation to communicate and for hunting their prey. Some dolphins are in danger of being extinct and a few states have outlawed the hunting of dolphins.
